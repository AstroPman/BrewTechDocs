## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login screen and select "Forgot your password?" Follow the instructions sent to your registered email address to reset your password.

### How do I update my app?

To ensure you have the latest version of Brew Tech, go to the App Store, search for Brew Tech, and tap "Update" if an update is available.

### How do I report a bug or issue?

If you encounter any issues or bugs, please email us at [brew.technology.japan@gmail.com](mailto:brew.technology.japan@gmail.com) with a detailed description of the problem. Screenshots and steps to reproduce the issue are very helpful.

### How do I change my account information?

To update your account information, open the app, navigate to the settings menu, and select "Account" tab. From there, you can update your personal details and preferences.

### Can I use Brew Tech offline?

No, Brew Tech does not keep any data on your device. You need to be online.

## User Guides

### Getting Started with Brew Tech

1. Download and install Brew Tech from the App Store.
2. Open the app and create a new account or log in with your existing credentials.
3. Explore the various features like recipe management, brew tracking, and more.

### Creating and Managing Recipes

- Navigate to the "Recipes" tab.
- Tap the "+" icon to create a new recipe.
- Enter the required details such as ingredients, steps, and brew method.
- Save your recipe and share it with others if desired.
